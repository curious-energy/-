**本博客名的来源** From wikipedia: The technological singularity – also, simply, the singularity – is a hypothetical future point in time at which technological growth becomes uncontrollable and irreversible, resulting in unforeseeable changes to human civilization. According to the most popular version of the singularity hypothesis, called intelligence explosion, an upgradable intelligent agent (such as a computer running software-based artificial general intelligence) will eventually enter a "runaway reaction" of self-improvement cycles, with each new and more intelligent generation appearing more and more rapidly, causing an "explosion" in intelligence and resulting in a powerful superintelligence that qualitatively far surpasses all human intelligence. 本博客名就是源于上面的名词，奇点（singularity），因为自小深受科幻小说，电影的影响，对我们这个世界的终极发展产生了很大的期望。我相信，作恶的不是技术，而是利用技术的人，不应该对技术本身抱怨，我们可以做的事就是利用这些技术做不违初心的事情。 恰逢人类对人工智能有了很大希翼的时候（此时正处于第三次人工智能浪潮上），给我有机会遇到如此接近这个梦想的时候，我迫不及待投身于此，为了自己的追求，也为了更加美好的未来，我们遥首盼望着奇点时代的来临......
